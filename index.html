Welcome to our page

We are a group of college students who follow study ICT. We have created this page to help you decide what game would be the best for you to play next, according to your preferences.

The concept is simple, you will answer each question by picking either yes or no. and do this until the end of the quiz. After this you will end up at a page on wich you get to choose between a few games. We will then link you to a physical and an online store where you can purchase the game you picked.
